Introduction

Welcome to ReelGrid. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our website and game. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any data we may collect.

Information We Collect

ReelGrid itself does not require you to create an account, nor do we directly collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name or email address to play the game.

We may collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the game experience, block malicious users, and understand how the application is used.

Third-Party Services & Cookies

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, primarily for analytics and serving advertisements.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site to help keep ReelGrid free. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • For more information on how Google collects and uses your data, please visit How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

Local Storage

ReelGrid uses your browser's Local Storage to save your game progress, statistics, and theme preferences locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
